1. Return Window
Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery.

2. Condition of Items
Returned products must be unused and in their original packaging.

3. Return Process
To initiate a return, contact our support team with your order number and reason for return.

4. Refund Method
Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7 business days of receiving the returned item.

5. Non-returnable Items
Certain items like gift cards, downloadable software, and personal care goods cannot be returned.

6. Shipping Costs
Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer unless the product is defective.

7. Exchanges
If you need to exchange a product for the same item, contact our support team.
